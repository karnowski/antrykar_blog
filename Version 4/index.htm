Welcome to Anthony R. Karnowski.com

Welcome to the official site of Anthony R. Karnowski. Here you will find information about my current and past projects, including my work as an author and as a member of the avante-garde musical outfit Citizen X. I'll do my best to keep this title page updated with the latest news surrounding my work, and I'll even update my journal fairly often with the usually-pointless drivel that seems to flow incessantly from my brain.

I hope you enjoy your visit to my site, and I welcome any and all suggestions.

Best regards,

Anthony Karnowski
