Welcome to Anthony R. Karnowski.com

Welcome to the official site of Anthony R. Karnowski. Here you will find information about my current and past projects, including my work as an author and as a member of the avante-garde musical outfit Citizen X. I'll do my best to keep this title page updated with the latest news surrounding my work, and I'll even update my journal fairly often with the usually-pointless drivel that seems to flow incessantly from my brain.

I hope you enjoy your visit to my site, and I welcome any and all suggestions. Please direct your thoughts here.

Best regards,

Anthony Karnowski

November 19, 2005

Writers for Relief

An author friend of mine, Davey Beauchamp, emailed just after the tragedy of Hurricane Katrina asking for contributions. He had decided to put together an anthology to help with the relief fund. I know he's been working on it like crazy since then, but the book is now finished and available. Davey managed to get some really great talent together on this anthology, and I'm almost as excited to be listed beside such authors as Larry Niven as I am to be helping the survivors of such a horrible tragedy.

If you are in any way interested in Speculative Fiction and would like to help hundreds of thousands of our brothers and sisters, please order a copy by clicking here. All proceeds go to the American Red Cross, and you'll get some great stories in the process.
