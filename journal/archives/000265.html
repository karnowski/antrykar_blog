March 14, 2004

As day breaks...

As day breaks and the sun climbs over the tops of the mountains, casting long, dark shadows across the valley floor, I awake to something unexpected. At some point during the night, my angry bowels grew quiet and fell into a place of peace. No more grumbling or groaning at the slightest of movements; all seems right in the world again...

OK, maybe it's a little much to say that everything is right in the world, but at least everything is right in my body!!!

Sorry, I'm sure none of you really care, but I was just so thrilled when I woke up for the first day since Wednesday without having to run straight into the bathroom...

Oh well...
