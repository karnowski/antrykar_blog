November 6, 2005

Bristol Comic Fest

The Bristol Comic Festival yesterday was great! Mike and Ben put on a killer show. For a first year con it was amazingly organized and well-promoted. I think the final count of attendees was just over 300, but it felt like there were a lot more people than that there.

As far as I know they're planning on doing it again next year, so watch them for details. If I'm right, we can expect next year to be an even bigger success.
