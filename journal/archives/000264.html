March 13, 2004

Blah...

That's about the only word I can think of to describe how I feel right now. Well, wait... really shitty might not be a bad one either. For the past... three days? maybe four, I have been abysmally sick. Diarrhea, nausea, all of the really fun sicknesses. I almost wish I could feel like this every day!

Ok, that's not even funny to joke about. I shouldn't tempt the fates like that. I've always been under the impression that stomach viruses should be gone after a couple of days, but this one has been hanging around like a champ.

I can almost see the little bugger clinging to the inside of my bowels, screaming in ecstacy everytime it manages to keep from being blown away...

oh well... sorry if none of you wanted to read about that... but I had to put something about it down in writing. Nothing seems real to me until I see it in the written (well, at least TYPED) word.

I just received an email from PublishAmerica, the company that will be printing my book later this year. Still no actual word on my book, but they did just hire 6 new editors, bringing their total number to 30. So, this means they are editing 30 books at a time. I wonder where mine is on the list?

As soon as I know more about it, I'll let you all know.

I'm off to bed again... may have to stop off at the Porcelin Temple first, though.... make my daily tithe...

someone please help me...
