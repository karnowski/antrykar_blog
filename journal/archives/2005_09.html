September 30, 2005

A Couple of Things

First off, I sent the cover for Path of Darkness off to the publisher today. It will be out in no more than six weeks. I'll be sure to post links to where you can find it as soon as I have them.

In other news, Matt Wenger has started illustrating Cameron and the Grim, my Young Adult story that should be out around the first of the year; maybe even sooner. The ones I've seen so far look great! I can't wait till he finishes.

And lastly, I watched the season premier of Smallville last night. I thought it was pretty good. I liked that the Fortress of Solitude looked very much like the one from the movies. I like that Lex has been firmly placed into the "villain" category now, but I really don't like how it ended. First off, they acted like Clark's never lost his powers before. I can't remember when it happened exactly, but I know it's happened. Plus, why can't they just let the whole Lana thing go? They've tried it and it doesn't work. Accept that. Lois is way hotter, anyway.

Oh well. I don't know why I brought that up; it must have just been on my mind.

That's about it for now. I have a full weekend of gigs and homework ahead of me.
