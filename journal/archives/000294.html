I'm Hooked

This whole blogging thing has become a sort of addiction for me now. I can't really explain it, but posting this--what amounts to rambling--stuff online and then having people read it and comment on it is great fun. I look forward to seeing who showed up and who had something to add. It's official: I'm hooked.

I watched a movie last night called Red Planet. It was all right. It's the kind of flick that, while watching, you think to yourself: "Man, this would make a great book." The film itself was very superficial as far as characters and plot were concerned. The premise was pretty cool, although totally unbelievable. Click here to see the IMDB entry about it.

So, that's all I have right now. Everyone have a nice day, and I'll try to write more later. That is, if I have anything worth writing about.
