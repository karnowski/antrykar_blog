April 4, 2005

Gig This Saturday

This Saturday, April 9th, Ron and I will be playing at AJ's in Maryville with Blue Revolution, a blues-based jam band. We're set to go on around 9 and will be playing till about 1 in the morning. I haven't been to AJ's yet, so I can't vouch for the quality of the establishment, but I know the musicians we're playing with are top-notch. If you find yourself in Maryville looking for some good music this Saturday, stop in and check it out!

There's not too much more to report right now. I'm still waiting to hear back from a couple of places I've submitted to, but as soon as I know something I'll report it here.

I've changed the name of the Forgotten Prophecies sequel again. It's not too different, but it's now called Path of Darkness. I'm about 2/5 of the way through the second draft and it's coming along nicely.

Anyway, that's all for now. Take care!
